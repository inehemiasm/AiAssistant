# Play Store Listing: Chevere AI

## Short Description
Private, local-first AI assistant. Chat and generate images entirely on-device.

## Full Description
Experience the future of artificial intelligence with **Chevere AI**—the assistant that respects your privacy by keeping your data where it belongs: on your phone.

Unlike traditional AI chatbots that rely on the cloud, Chevere AI performs neural inference locally. Your prompts, images, and conversations never leave your device unless you choose to share them.

### Key Features:
*   **Local-First Privacy:** Chat with advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) like Gemma 4 Edge without an internet connection for core reasoning.
*   **On-Device Image Generation:** Create stunning visuals using local diffusion models. No subscription fees, no cloud queues.
*   **Vision-Aware Chat:** Attach images and ask Chevere AI to describe, analyze, or extract information from them using on-device vision capabilities.
*   **Hardware Optimized:** Specifically tuned for modern mobile chipsets to provide high-efficiency, low-latency responses.
*   **Zero Data Tracking:** We don't build profiles on you. No chat logs are sent to a central server.

### Why Local AI?
*   **Offline Access:** Use your assistant in "airplane mode," in remote areas, or anywhere without a signal.
*   **Security:** Eliminate the risk of data breaches on external servers.
*   **Control:** You choose which models to download and when to delete your history.

*Note: Initial setup requires downloading a "Neural Core" (AI model) which can range from 1GB to 4GB. A high-speed Wi-Fi connection and modern hardware are recommended for the best experience.*
